Hi, my name is

Abel Soares Siqueira

I’m a Research Software Engineer at the Netherlands eScience Center since 2021. My current main interests are Code Quality, the Julia Programming Language, and Mathematical Optimization. I had a chance to work with Typescript/Vue for frontend, a bit of C++ tooling, Graph neural networks in Python, Kubernetes, and modeling electricity markets with Julia/JuMP.

I am a YouTuber and streamer. Since 2020, I talk about software development, mostly using the Julia programming language. Some of my videos are purely about teaching Julia, but some are related to some mathematical field, usually optimization.

I have PhD in nonlinear optimization and was a professor from 2014 to 2021 at the Federal University of Paran√° (Brazil). I taught multivariate calculus, numerical methods, optimization, and data science. I was involved in the Data Science & Big Data specialization. I was co-creator of CiDAMO, a Data Science, Machine Learning, and Optimization group. My research revolved mainly around numerical optimization in the Julia language. I am co-creator of the Julia Smooth Optimizers organization, which holds more than 50 Julia packages related to optimization and Linear Algebra.
